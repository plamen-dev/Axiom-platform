## What belongs here

- Durable cross-program decisions and their rationale.
- Standing directions that change how programs operate.
- Communications that must survive outside chat threads and remain authoritative.

## What does not belong here

- Routine chat messages and working back-and-forth.
- Traceability records (those live in `docs/logs/`).
- Artifacts, recordings, screenshots, or Devin session dumps.

## Recording rule

A communication becomes canonical here only when it represents a **durable
decision or standing direction**. Record it via PR with: date, originating
program, decision/direction, rationale, and affected programs. Keep it concise.
